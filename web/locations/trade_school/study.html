From level 8 and up, you can start learning additional skills. Each skill unlocks an ability which you can perform using the /skill menu.

Your proficiency in a skill is determined by the number of courses you have completed in that skill.

Courses are offered at the Trade School, in the Outpost. Each time you level up, starting when you reach L8, you can complete one additional course.
