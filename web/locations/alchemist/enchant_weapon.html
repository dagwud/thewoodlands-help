Enchant Weapon

Enchanted weapon have been imbued with magical properties.

Creatures that have unenchanted immunity are immune to all weapons that have not been enchanted.

To enchant a Weapon, go to the Alchemist. You need to provide the weapon to be enchanted, as well as a magical trinket, such as a potion, from which the magical essence will be extracted.

See: Immunity, Trinkets
