Equip Item

Equipping an item readies if for active use.

Think of it like this: youâ€™re carrying a bunch of stuff in a bag on your back/strapped to your waist/etc. Equipping an item means taking it out and holding it in one of your hands.

You can only attack with weapons that are equipped, and a shield will only protect you if it is equipped.

Equipping certain trinkets, such as potions, will cause you to consume them. Equipping a Potion of Healing, for example, will cause you to drink the potion, gaining hit points back. At that point, the potion has been used and will disappear from your inventory.

To equip an item, use the /inv command and click the /e link next to the appropriate item.
